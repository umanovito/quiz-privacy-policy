Privacy Policy

Effective date: 22 July 2025

Thank you for choosing to be part of our community at Individual Entrepreneur Vitalii Iushmanov (“Company,” “we,” “us,” or “our”). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you use Quizzz (the “App”) and any related services.

TL;DR — We collect only the data needed to run quizzes, handle purchases, show (or hide) ads, and—if you allow—publish your score on the global leaderboard. We never sell your data.

2. How we collect data

  • You provide it directly – e.g., by entering a nickname or uploading an avatar.
  • Automatically – via SDKs such as Apple StoreKit (purchases), Firebase Analytics (usage), and AdMob/Appodeal (ads, only if ads are enabled).
  • From Apple – we receive purchase receipts and subscription status through StoreKit 2 server notifications.

3. Why we use your data

4. Global Leaderboard (opt-in)

We upload only your nickname, total score, and optional avatar after you enable the toggle “Share my scores globally.”

  • Disable it anytime in Settings → Privacy → Leaderboard.
  • Your data will be removed from public boards within 24 hours after you opt out.

5. Advertising & Analytics

  • Ads – The free version shows banner/interstitial ads via Google AdMob/Appodeal. The IDFA is used only after you grant App Tracking Transparency (ATT) consent.
  • Turn Off Ads – Purchasing the corresponding product disables third-party ads and IDFA usage.
  • Analytics – We use Firebase Analytics and Apple in-app analytics to understand aggregate usage. No personal profiling.

You can reset or limit ad tracking in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

7. How we protect your data

  • TLS 1.2+ encryption in transit.
  • Hashing & tokenisation of user identifiers.
  • ISO-27001 certified cloud hosting (DigitalOcean / Google Cloud).

9. Children’s privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we learn that we have done so without parental consent, we will delete such data immediately.

10. International data transfers

Your information may be processed on servers outside your country. We rely on Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent safeguards for transfers outside the EEA/UK.

11. Subscriptions & Purchases

We process your Apple transaction ID, product ID, price tier, and encrypted receipt token solely to validate and renew your auto-renewable subscription. All payment processing is handled by Apple; we never see or store your full payment-card details.
