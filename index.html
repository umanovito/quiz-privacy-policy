2. How We Collect Data

  • You provide it – for example, by entering a nickname or signing up with e-mail and password.
  • Via sign-in providers – we receive your basic profile ID and e-mail (if shared) from Apple ID, Google Sign-In, or Facebook Login when you authenticate.
  • Automatically – through Apple StoreKit (purchases), Firebase Analytics (usage), and AdMob/Appodeal (ads).
  • From Apple servers – StoreKit 2 server notifications about subscription status.

4. Leaderboard (Opt-in)

We upload your nickname, score and optional avatar only after you enable the switch “Share my scores globally”. You can disable it anytime in Settings → Privacy → Leaderboard; your data disappears from public boards within 24 hours.

5. Advertising & Analytics

  • Ads – Banner/interstitial ads are shown via Google AdMob/Appodeal. IDFA is used only after you grant App Tracking Transparency (ATT) consent.
  • Turn Off Ads – Purchasing the related product removes third-party ads and stops IDFA usage.
  • Analytics – Firebase Analytics collects aggregated, de-identified usage metrics. No personal profiling.
